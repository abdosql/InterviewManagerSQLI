# Possible Read Operations for Interview Management System

1. User-related queries:
   - GetUserById(id)
   - GetUserByUsername(username)
   - GetUserByEmail(email)
   - GetAllUsers()
   - GetUserNotifications(userId)

2. Candidate-related queries:
   - GetCandidateById(id)
   - GetAllCandidates()
   - GetCandidateCV(candidateId)
   - GetCandidatePhases(candidateId)
   - GetCandidateInterviews(candidateId)

3. Evaluator-related queries:
   - GetEvaluatorById(id)
   - GetAllEvaluators()
   - GetEvaluatorInterviews(evaluatorId)
   - GetEvaluatorsBySpecialization(specialization)

4. HR Manager-related queries:
   - GetHRManagerById(id)
   - GetAllHRManagers()
   - GetHRManagerInterviews(hrManagerId)
   - GetHRManagersByDepartment(department)

5. Interview-related queries:
   - GetInterviewById(id)
   - GetAllInterviews()
   - GetInterviewsByDate(date)
   - GetInterviewsByLocation(location)
   - GetInterviewAppreciations(interviewId)
   - GetInterviewAgenda(interviewId)
   - GetInterviewStatus(interviewId)
   - GetInterviewsByCandidateId(candidateId)
   - GetInterviewsByEvaluatorId(evaluatorId)
   - GetInterviewsByHRManagerId(hrManagerId)

6. Appreciation-related queries:
   - GetAppreciationById(id)
   - GetAllAppreciations()
   - GetAppreciationsByInterviewId(interviewId)
   - GetAppreciationsByScore(score)

7. Agenda-related queries:
   - GetAgendaById(id)
   - GetAllAgendas()
   - GetAgendaByInterviewId(interviewId)
   - GetAgendaByDateRange(startDate, endDate)
   - GetAgendaByStatus(status)

8. Notification-related queries:
   - GetNotificationById(id)
   - GetAllNotifications()
   - GetNotificationsByUserId(userId)
   - GetNotificationsByDateRange(startDate, endDate)

9. Candidate Phase-related queries:
   - GetCandidatePhaseById(id)
   - GetAllCandidatePhases()
   - GetCandidatePhasesByCandidateId(candidateId)
   - GetCandidatePhasesByPhase(phase)
   - GetCandidatePhasesByDateRange(startDate, endDate)

10. Interview Status-related queries:
    - GetInterviewStatusById(id)
    - GetAllInterviewStatuses()
    - GetInterviewStatusesByInterviewId(interviewId)
    - GetInterviewStatusesByStatus(status)
    - GetInterviewStatusesByDateRange(startDate, endDate)

11. CV-related queries:
    - GetCVById(id)
    - GetAllCVs()
    - GetCVByCandidateId(candidateId)

12. Cross-entity queries:
    - GetUpcomingInterviews(date)
    - GetPendingAppreciations()
    - GetCandidatesInPhase(phase)
    - GetEvaluatorsWithUpcomingInterviews(date)
    - GetHRManagersWithPendingInterviews()
    - GetRecentlyCompletedInterviews(date)
    - GetTopRatedCandidates()
    - GetMostActiveEvaluators(dateRange)
    - GetInterviewStatisticsByDateRange(startDate, endDate)
